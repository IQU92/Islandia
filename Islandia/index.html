About Islandia, Inc.

Established on June 30, 1988, Islandia, Inc. is a family-operated business based in South Florida with a solid reputation for delivering high-quality work across various commercial and institutional projects.

Our projects include office buildings, hotels, hospitals, schools, warehouses, and retail centers throughout the region.
